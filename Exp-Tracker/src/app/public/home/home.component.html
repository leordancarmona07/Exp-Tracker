EXPENSES TRACKER

Helps you keep a list of your expenses. Helps you analyze your expenses more appropriately. Helps you save more!

ABOUT

An expense tracker is a web-based application that keeps track of one’s daily expenses. It has personal finance dashboard for whoever signs up. It tracks and categorizes one’s expenses and from there, creates a pie graph with weekly and monthly basis. It also summarizes the yearly expenses of the user. It also provides an expense report for the user.
